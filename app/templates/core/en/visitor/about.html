Out story

It all started one summer when we decided to find ourselves jobs and internships in order to learn new things and gain more experience. All of the doors were closed for us just because we were “young” and didn’t “have any experience”... At the beginning of the school year we noticed that our classmates are dealing with the same problem.

That’s why we decided our system needed to change. After a lot of thinking and hanging out in the sitting room of the Sofia High School of Mathematics we ended up with a solution - Headstarter. This is a platform which provides a centralized place for ambitious students who are looking for work experience and for companies who are on the search for their new young and motivated talents.
