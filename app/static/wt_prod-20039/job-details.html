  • New York, NY, USA

  • Post Date: May 29, 2015

  • www.demolink.org

We’re looking for a full stack marketer who can master social media, copywriting, photography, video editing, community outreach and more. Are you entrepreneurial by nature and a doer with strong examples of execution? Are you a highly energetic and a self starter? If the answer is “yes”, then we need you!

Job Responsibilities

  • Increasing our social media following to 1M+ within the next 18 months
  • Coming up with new, impactful SMM ideas
  • Snapping beautiful photos for Instagram, Facebook and Snapchat
  • Travelling to events, running demos and stands

Why Work With us

  • Growth opportunity in one of the USA’s and Europe's fastest growing marketing agencies
  • Being a part of a friendly and qualified team
  • You’ll have a chance to work with industry leaders and renowned advertising experts
  • Free healthcare and entertainment options
